The Wise Old Owl

In the heart of the forest, there lived a wise old owl
With feathers soft and brown, and eyes that shone like jewels

She perched on the tallest tree, and watched the world below

One day, a curious rabbit came, and asked the owl for aid

The owl listened carefully, and thought for a moment then
She gave the rabbit her advice, and the rabbit left with a grin
